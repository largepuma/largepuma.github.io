I can't believe it that I've worked for 4 years!  How fast it is!
<!-- more -->
First one and a half years I worked at Ningbo Fotile Corporation as a network engineer, and next two and a half years I'm at  Archermind Technology as a software engineer. What did I do these years? It seems that I did a lot. In Ningbo we first had a training about career and the firm. HR director Pan Jiuan's knowledge and experience told us lots of things. We had choice to work in workshop of plant, we learned how to assemble range hoods and hobs. Later, I came to Ningbo Sales Company to assist productions sales and learned how to sell to others what they wanted. After the training, I got into IT department,  studied IT support and ERP development. About one year later, I felt it's boring here so ready to try other choice, and I got an invitation to Nanjing from Suxing who was my colleague in Ningbo. I received it. After arriving at Nanjing, I started my career of programmer. I learned lots of new things, iterative development, test, release, project management, webkit, browser, android, c++, java, out source, and so. I experienced several projects, and I know what's my future. Four years have passed. I will try every possibility. I will try to be a entrepreneur, dive into the internet ocean. I will try to learn UE / UI, try to learn product management. I will try to create something that will affect people's life. I will try to invent something that never existed, and great. I will to be good to my friends and myself.
