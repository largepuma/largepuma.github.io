If you observe the society, you would find lots jobs disappeared, and lots new jobs are created.
<!-- more -->
Here, I will list some jobs that is disappearing in China.

1.  driver: Google Auto-driving Car
2.  bus ticket seller: you should buy the ticket by yourself
3.  bank seller: Too many ATMs on the street
4.  translator: Google translate, machine translation

And at the same time, lots of new careers are created.

1.  abstractor: responsible to get the mode of anything (this job is created by myself)
2.  implementer: implement the new mode to reality

In the future, you still should learn lots knowledge that we have known. But it's only the basis for you to do other things that we don't know now.
